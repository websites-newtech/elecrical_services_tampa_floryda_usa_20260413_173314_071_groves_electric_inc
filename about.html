Meet the Owner

Chris Groves
Master Electrician

"Hello, I am Chris Groves — Owner of Groves Electric, Inc. I am a licensed, bonded and insured Master Electrician and the owner of a thriving electrical contracting business in South Tampa.

After obtaining a degree in Electrical Engineering and putting in many years of hard work, I started my own electrical contracting company in Minneapolis in 1998. Six years later I relocated Groves Electric to South Tampa. Since then I have grown the business with two additional crews of talented and experienced electricians who share my commitment to customer satisfaction and quality workmanship.

There are a number of things that set us apart from our competitors. We give free quotes. We never charge a trip charge or any other hidden fees. We guarantee our customer's satisfaction with every job. We warranty our parts and labor for a full year. Our electricians are highly skilled with an average of 15 years of experience.

The most important difference is our philosophy — We pride ourselves in doing quality work at a fair price and always being considerate of our customer's home and their time. We show up when we say we will and we clean up after ourselves.

As the owner and director of operations, I am personally involved in each project my company does. When you call Groves Electric, you are calling me directly on my cell phone! I ensure that the work performed is not just to code, but that every possible safety consideration is put in place. I look forward to earning your business."
